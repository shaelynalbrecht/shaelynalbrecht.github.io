Baton Twirling

Twirling has shaped me into the person I am today. It's taught me to be diligent, dedicated, and detail-oriented. I've grown creatively through learning and creating choreography that people will enjoy watching; I've also grown as a communicator and collaborator through teamwork and through teaching twirling lessons to my own students.

I'm grateful for this sport and the lifelong friendships I've found through twirling. I'm also grateful for the opportunities twirling has led me to, including competing at the international level and performing with the best marching band in the world.

I'm honored to have twirled with the Michigan Marching Band for the past four years and to have represented the University of Michigan as the 2017 National Collegiate Twirling Champion.
