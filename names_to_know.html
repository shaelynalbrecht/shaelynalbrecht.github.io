Names to Know

If you're looking to see what twirling is all about, or if you're a twirler yourself, these are some twirlers you're definitely going to want to know about.

Stacy Singer

A seven time world champion, her speed, energy, and abilities are incredible!

Hollie Neilson

The creator of the popular "Holliewood" neck roll, Hollie is a beautiful twirler.

Jennifer Marcus 3-Baton

Before she toured with Cirque du Soleil, she toured the world at numerous world championships and is a phenomenal multiple baton twirler.

Chelsea Russell

Former Miss Majorette of America, world champion, and University of Iowa Golden Girl, she's a stunning example of what it means to be a baton twirler.

Tomoe Nishigaki

Reigning senior women's world champion, Tomoe executes every single move effortlessly and beautifully.

Keisuke Komada

He received perfect 10's in every round of his final worlds competition. That speaks for itself!!

Karrissa Wimberley

She's won many national and world championships, and her athleticism is obvious in her incredible performances. Search #PlayBatonWithKarrissa on social media, and you'll find her encouraging twirlers to try new tricks and have fun.

Team Japan

Team France

Annetta Lucero

Completely innovative and pushing the limits of our sport, Annetta is inspirational and enourages twirlers to twirl strong.

Savannah Miller

She's been at the top of both NBTA and USTA, and she's a complete powerhouse. She also twirls for the University of Arkansas!

Mark Nash

His technique is gorgeous, and he's well known now as a coach and judge.

Seishi Inagaki

Phenomenal twirler and phenomenal choreographer: from being a world champion to creating world championship-winning routines, Seishi is amazing.
